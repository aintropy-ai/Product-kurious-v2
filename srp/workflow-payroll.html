Workflow

Payroll & Timesheet Workflow

From hours logged to payment deposited

1
End of Pay Period TRIGGER
Triggered automatically at end of pay period, or when an employee says "submit my timesheet" in Kuri.
2
Pull Hours & Pre-fill Timesheet AUTO
Agent pulls hours from connected tools (calendar, time tracker, project management) and pre-fills the timesheet for the employee.
3
Check Hours Against Threshold AUTO
Agent evaluates the submitted hours against standard thresholds to determine routing.
IF 35–42 hrs → auto-approved, sent to finance.
IF > 42 hrs → flagged for manager (overtime).
IF < 35 hrs → flagged as unusual.
IF 0 hrs → check if employee is on PTO.
4
Manager Reviews (If Flagged) APPROVAL NEEDED
Manager reviews flagged timesheets in Kuri. Shows: "[Name] logged 45 hrs. 3 over standard. Approve overtime?"
IF approved → sent to finance for processing.
IF rejected → employee asked to revise and resubmit.
5
Finance Verifies & Initiates Payroll AUTO
Finance team verifies the approved timesheets and initiates the payroll run.
6
Admin Approves Payment Release APPROVAL NEEDED
Admin reviews and approves the final payment release. Agents never move money without human sign-off.
Shows total amount and full breakdown. Agents never move money.
7
ACH Deposit Processed AUTO
Payment is processed via ACH. Typically completes in 1–3 business days.
8
Payslip Generated & Stored AUTO
Payslip is automatically generated with full breakdown and securely stored in the employee's records.
9
Employee Notified AUTO
Employee receives notification: "Your March payslip is ready. View it in your profile."
10
Timesheet Reminder PROACTIVE
If timesheet not submitted 2 days before deadline, agent sends: "You haven't submitted your timesheet. Payroll won't process without it."

Standard hours auto-approved. Overtime flagged. Payment requires admin sign-off. Always.
