Technical blogs and HowTos. Research white papers and conference papers. HuggingFace leaderboards with ranks relevant to their domain. Mindshare blogs that attract attention.

Open source templates and repos on GitHub. Engineers clone, run, see results before talking to anyone. Documentation and self-help available.

🌐

4. They Land on the Website

One-click access for all personas. Clear pricing, plans, and feature limits. AEO (AI Engine Optimization) brings them organically.

5. They Try a Live Demo

Multiple live demos to interact with. NJ Open Data (85M records), Legal Video (4,000 hrs). Different use cases, go deeper on each. No signup required.

📊

6. They Verify Our Claims

Published benchmarks on HuggingFace anyone can check. NJ Open Data leaderboard, ChemRAG leaderboard. They trust us because they verified it themselves.
